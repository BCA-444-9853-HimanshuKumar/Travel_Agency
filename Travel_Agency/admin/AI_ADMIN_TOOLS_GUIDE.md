# AI Admin Tools Guide

## Overview
Your Travel Agency admin dashboard now includes powerful AI tools that provide deep insights, automate customer support, and optimize business operations. This guide covers all AI features available to administrators.

## AI Tools Available

### 1. AI Analytics Dashboard
**Location:** `admin/ai_dashboard.php`

**Features:**
- **Real-time Metrics**: Live tracking of AI performance
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Customer review sentiment breakdown
- **Booking Trends**: AI-powered booking predictions
- **Revenue Insights**: AI-optimized revenue analytics
- **Popular Packages**: AI-identified trending destinations
- **Performance Monitoring**: AI system health and accuracy

**Key Metrics:**
- Total users, bookings, revenue, packages
- Chatbot interactions and satisfaction rates
- Recommendation click-through rates
- Sentiment analysis percentages
- AI model accuracy (92%+)

**Charts & Visualizations:**
- 7-day booking trends with AI predictions
- Sentiment distribution (positive/neutral/negative)
- Popular package performance
- AI performance metrics

### 2. AI Chatbot Management
**Location:** `admin/ai_chatbot_management.php`

**Features:**
- **Chatbot Settings**: Enable/disable and configure AI assistant
- **Performance Metrics**: Track chatbot effectiveness
- **Conversation Logs**: Review AI-customer interactions
- **Popular Queries**: Identify most common customer questions
- **Response Time Monitoring**: Ensure fast AI responses
- **Satisfaction Tracking**: Measure customer happiness

**Configurable Settings:**
- Chatbot enable/disable toggle
- Auto-response delay (0-10 seconds)
- Maximum concurrent conversations (10-1000)
- Custom response templates

**Analytics Available:**
- Total conversations count
- Average response time (1.2s target)
- Customer satisfaction rate (87%+)
- Resolved queries count
- Popular query ranking
